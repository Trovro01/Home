The person behind Trovro
Om Kumbhar
Founder & CEO

"Every electrician, cleaner and stylist in this city is running a small business. Trovro exists so they keep everything they earn — and so the family down the street can find them in thirty seconds."

Om started Trovro in Mumbai after watching skilled local professionals lose a third of their income to platforms meant to help them. He builds the product end to end — the Android app, the verification, the way people get paid — around a single rule: the person doing the work comes first, and the neighbourhood they serve comes a close second.
