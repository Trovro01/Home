How it works

Three steps. No middleman.

Trovro introduces you and then gets out of the way. Money never passes through us — which is why we don't take a cut of it.

Step 01

Find someone nearby

Pick a service and see professionals who actually cover your locality — not someone three suburbs away. Every profile shows their photo, skills, service areas and rating before you say a word.

Step 02

Book and chat

Send a booking request. Your pro accepts, and you sort out the timing and the job in the in-app chat. You can follow the request from pending to accepted to done.

Step 03

Pay them directly

Cash or UPI, settled between you and the professional. Then rate the work. That rating is public, and it's what keeps the next person safe.

For professionals

Keep every rupee you earn.

Other platforms take 15–30% of your bill, then quietly raise the customer's price to cover it. Trovro takes nothing. The customer pays you. That's the whole model.

Om Kumbhar · Mumbai, India

"The plumber who fixed our tap was good. He was also invisible — no way for anyone to find him except a slip on a wall. I didn't want to take a cut from him. I wanted to make him findable."

Om Kumbhar is the founder and CEO of Trovro, which he designs, builds and runs single-handedly from Mumbai while still a student. He wrote every screen of the app, reviews every identity verification himself, and answers the support email personally.

Trovro operates on a 0% commission model by conviction, not as a launch offer. Commissions get passed back to customers as higher prices and to workers as thinner margins. Trovro's job is introductions and trust — the money stays between the two people who earned it.
